Electrical engineering

Introduction

The Bachelor's course in Electrical Engineering in Da Vinci University will give you a great start in this exciting field. With our great team of teachers, you'll grow your knowledge in the field of Electrical Engineering. You can start the study with no prior knowledge of our experienced team will guide you through the course answering all of your questions.

Content

Year one - foundation phase. In the first half, you'll get to know the field of Electrical engineering gets used to the study load. Year two - The focus is shifted more towards the specific subjects in the study: embedded systems and power systems Year three - this year you'll get to experience the field by going on an internship. Year four - You'll go on an exchange course - you can choose the country of interest.
